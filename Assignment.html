Othniel Nuu-gwor Nana Korjo Toomann Sr.

Othniel Nuugwor Toomann known by stage name Onto-Boi is a Ghanaian-Liberian Recording Artist | Song-writer | Music Producer | Journalist | Computer Programmer | Entrepreneur | Software Developer. Born in Accra, Ghana to Liberian parents and raised in Liberia, Onto-Boi hailed from the Central Province of Bong County, Suakoko District. His nice tune, captivating lyrics and spontaneous rhythms authenticate his sound as one of the very best Afrobeats / AfroPop Artist in Liberia.
